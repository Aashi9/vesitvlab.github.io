Objectives:

  • Students will be able to plot the graph of intrinsic absorption, extrinsic absorption and Rayleigh scattering of optical fiber for different material and different wavelength of Optical signal.
  • Students will be able to analyze the graph.
  • Students will be able to understand the impact of length of fiber on the attenuation characteristics of fiber.
  • Students will be able to identify most suitable wavelength for transmission.
  • Students will be able to evaluate the characteristics of the different fiber and identify the suitable one for transmission.
